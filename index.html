Bozi Company

Bozi Company is a digital support service that helps creators verify YouTube subscriptions in a simple and reliable way. Our goal is to provide a safe, transparent, and user-friendly experience for both creators and users.

Why We Request User Information

We may request limited user information solely for the purpose of verifying YouTube subscriptions and preventing misuse of the service. The information is used only to operate and improve the service and is not shared with third parties.
